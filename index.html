How to Use LineArt Maps

1
Upload your GPS file

Drop a GPX, TCX, or FIT file onto the upload area — or click to browse. These files contain the GPS route from your run, ride, or hike.

2
Get your GPS file

Strava: Activity page → ··· menu → Export GPX
Garmin: Activity → gear icon → Export to GPX
Apple Watch: Use the HealthFit app to export workouts

3
Customize your design

Choose a color preset or create your own. Adjust line width, smoothing, and style. Toggle the city map background and elevation profile. Edit text overlays — title, date, and stats are added automatically.

4
Export and print

Download as PNG (high-res), SVG (vector — best for large prints), or PDF (exact paper dimensions). Take it to any print shop or upload to an online printing service.

💡
Tips

Longer routes with more GPS points produce more detailed art. Dark backgrounds with light routes look stunning as prints. Use smoothing for a more artistic feel. For large prints (A2+), export as SVG for the sharpest quality.

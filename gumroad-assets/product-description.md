# Your Best Routes Deserve a Spot on Your Wall

Every run, ride, and hike tells a story. LineArt Maps turns the GPS data from your favorite activities into stunning minimalist line art — ready to print and frame.

**Upload. Customize. Print. That's it.**

## What You Get

A downloadable app that runs entirely in your browser — no install, no account, no subscription. Just open the file and start creating.

- **Upload any GPS file** — GPX, TCX, or FIT formats from Strava, Garmin, Apple Watch, Wahoo, Komoot, AllTrails, and more
- **Customize everything** — Choose from 10 beautiful color presets or pick your own colors. Adjust line width, style (solid, dashed, dotted), and smoothing
- **Add text overlays** — Activity name, date, distance, or any custom text. Drag to position, choose from 8 fonts
- **Export print-ready files** — PNG (high-res), SVG (vector), or PDF with exact paper dimensions
- **All paper sizes** — A5 through A0, portrait or landscape

## FAQ

**How do I get my GPS file?**
Export from Strava (three dots menu → Export GPX), Garmin Connect (gear icon → Export), or any fitness app. The README includes step-by-step instructions for all major platforms.

**What paper sizes are supported?**
A5, A4, A3, A2, A1, and A0 — in both portrait and landscape orientation.

**Can I use it on my phone?**
Yes! Open index.html in your mobile browser. The interface adapts to smaller screens.

**Do I need to install anything?**
No. Just unzip, open index.html, and you're ready to go. No Node.js, no Python, no command line.

**Can I modify the code?**
Yes, for your personal use. The license allows personal modifications. See LICENSE for details.

**Is there a subscription?**
No. One payment, yours forever. No accounts, no tracking, no recurring fees.
